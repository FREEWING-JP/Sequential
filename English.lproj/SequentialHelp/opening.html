Opening documents sequentially

You can quickly open the next folder, archive or PDF in a series after finishing with the one before it.

To quickly open the next document:

  1. Leave the current document open.
  2. Choose File > Openâ€¦ or press Command-O. The current document is selected, and you can simply press the down arrow and then Enter.

When you open a second document, the first one is automatically closed. Sequential is not able to open more than one document at a time.
