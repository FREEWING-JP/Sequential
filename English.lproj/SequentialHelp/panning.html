Viewing large images

Large images can be scaled down to fit on the screen or scrolled across in several different ways.

To scroll across large images:

  • Drag with the mouse.
  • Use the navigation keys (arrows, Home, End, Page Up, and Page Dn).
  • Press space, V, B or C.
  • Press 0 or Enter (on the number pad only).
  • Press 1-9 (on the number pad only) to scroll a screenful in the appropriate direction.

Pressing space or 0 causes Sequential scroll across the image if possible, or other wise down to the next line. Pressing V or Enter causes Sequential to scroll down the image if possible, or otherwise to the beginning of the next column.

Hold Option to reverse the direction for any of these four keys. B and C are equivalent to Option-space and Option-V, respectively.

Scroll bars are not displayed.
