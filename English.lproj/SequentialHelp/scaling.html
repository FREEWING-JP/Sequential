Scaling modes

The scaling mode determines how large images are displayed. There are three modes:

  • Actual Size: Displays the image normally with no scaling.
  • Fit to Screen: Scales images so that no scrolling is required.
  • Automatic Fit: Scales wide images to fit vertically, and tall images to fit horizontally.

For the Fit to Screen and Automatic Fit modes, you can choose whether to allow images to be upscaled, downscaled, or scaled freely (both). Resolution independent images (such as PDFs) as always scaled freely.

You can also choose to apply a constant scaling factor.
