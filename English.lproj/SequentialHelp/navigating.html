Navigating between images

You can move between images in several different ways.

To go to the next or previous image:

  • Choose Page > Next or Page > Previous.
  • Click, Secondary-click, or Option-click anywhere on the image or background.
  • Press + or - (on the number pad only).
  • Scroll past the left or right edges of the current image.

To jump to a specific image:

  • If you wish to jump to the first or last image, choose Page > First or Page > Last.
  • Choose the desired image from the Page menu.

If you try to move past the first or last image, Sequential displays a graphical alert.
