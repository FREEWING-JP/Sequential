Pausing and resuming sessions

You can stop viewing at any time and quickly return to the same page later.

To bookmark the current page and stop viewing:

  1. Choose File > Pause or press Escape.

Pausing adds the page to the top of the Resume menu and closes the document.

When you wish to resume viewing the page, select it from the Resume menu. The image will be reopened and the pause session removed.
