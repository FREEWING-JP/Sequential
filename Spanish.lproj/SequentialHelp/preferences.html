Preferences

Setting Description
Antialias when upscaling Smooths images when they are drawn at larger than actual size. Images drawn smaller are always smoothed.
Animate images Play animated GIFs.
Fit windows to initial image size Automatically resize windows when the first image is loaded to fit.
Background The color and pattern displayed around images.
Full screen on The display to use in full screen mode.
Max folder depth The number of levels down into folders to open.
Primary click goes Determines the behavior of primary and secondary clicks. “To next page” can be either left or right depending on the reading direction, whereas “left” and “right” can be either the next or previous page.
